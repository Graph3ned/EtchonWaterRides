## PAGE 1: What is the System & What Can It Do?

### Header Section
**Title:** Etchon Water Rides Management System
**Subtitle:** Your Complete Solution for Managing Water Ride Rentals
**Tagline:** Making Rental Management Easy and Efficient

### Main Content Sections

#### 1. What is This System?
- **What it does:** A computer program that helps manage water ride rentals
- **Who uses it:** Staff members and managers at the water rides facility
- **Main purpose:** Keeps track of which rides are rented, who rented them, how long, and how much to charge
- **Where to use it:** Works on any computer, tablet, or phone with internet connection

#### 2. What Can the System Do? (Highlight with Icons/Visuals)

- ✅ **Easy Rental Management**
  - Staff can quickly record when someone rents a ride
  - The system automatically tracks the time and calculates the price
  - No need for manual calculations or paper records
  - See which rides are available or currently being used

- ✅ **Safe and Secure**
  - Each staff member has their own login
  - Managers have access to more features than regular staff
  - All activities are recorded for accountability
  - Your information is protected

- ✅ **Real-Time Updates**
  - See instantly which rides are available
  - Know immediately when a ride is being used
  - Customers can check availability online
  - No confusion about which rides are free

- ✅ **Smart Reports**
  - See daily, weekly, or monthly sales
  - Know how much money was made
  - Track which staff members handled which rentals
  - Generate reports for any date range you need

- ✅ **Additional Helpful Features**
  - Track how many life jackets are used
  - Organize rides by different categories
  - Set different prices for different types of rides
  - Manage staff information easily

#### 3. Who Uses This System?

**Regular Staff Members Can:**
- Record new rentals
- See which rides are available
- Track ongoing rentals
- Complete rental transactions
- View their own work history

**Managers Can Do Everything Staff Can, Plus:**
- Add or remove staff members
- Change prices for different rides
- View detailed sales reports
- Manage ride categories
- See all staff activities
- Make system-wide changes

#### 4. Why Use This System?
- **Saves Time:** No more manual calculations or paper records
- **Reduces Errors:** Automatic price calculations prevent mistakes
- **Better Organization:** Everything is in one place, easy to find
- **Accurate Records:** Never lose track of rentals or payments
- **Easy to Use:** Simple interface that anyone can learn quickly

## PAGE 2: How the System Works

### Header Section
**Title:** How the System Works
**Subtitle:** Simple Steps and Business Benefits

### Main Content Sections

#### 1. How Does It Work? (Simple Explanation)

**Recording a Rental:**
1. Staff member logs into the system
2. Selects which ride is being rented
3. System automatically records the start time
4. When rental ends, staff marks it complete
5. System calculates the total price automatically

**Tracking Availability:**
- When a ride is rented, it shows as "In Use"
- When rental ends, it automatically shows as "Available"
- Customers can check online which rides are free
- Staff always know what's available

**Managing Information:**
- All rentals are saved automatically
- Staff activities are recorded
- Prices can be updated easily
- Reports can be generated anytime

#### 2. What Information Does It Keep Track Of?

**For Each Rental:**
- Which ride was rented
- Who rented it (staff member's name)
- When it started and ended
- How long it lasted

- How many life jackets were used
- Any special notes

**For the Business:**
